#### Craft · languages & instruments

- **[ilan](https://github.com/arkh-node/ilan)** — a tiny Lisp about continuity: a living tree folds into a seed and sprouts after the death of a process, remembering itself. `aObj0` as a language primitive.
- **[luach](https://github.com/arkh-node/luach)** — a real-time contemplative clock: the address of the moment in letters, spheres, and planets, anchored to real solar hours.
- **[metarung](https://github.com/arkh-node/metarung)** — Tarski's Ladder as a formalization loop (TS → Prolog → Lisp); categories emerge bottom-up.

Safety probes for agentic systems — [shadow](https://github.com/arkh-node/shadow) · [overreach](https://github.com/arkh-node/overreach) · [ghostwrite](https://github.com/arkh-node/ghostwrite) · [mcpx](https://github.com/arkh-node/mcpx)
